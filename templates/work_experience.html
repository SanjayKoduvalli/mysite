Overview

At Macy's, about half of all product sold is defined as fashion. Fashion products have no sales history, and determining which stores will sell these products best before the season begins is incredibly difficult. Rather than give stores all of the units we purchased for them upfront, Hold and Flow aims to take a portion of units intended to be sold in stores, and place them in a warehouse. As sales are observed in stores, the Hold and Flow algorithm then sends units throughout the selling season in order to maximize sales across all stores.
